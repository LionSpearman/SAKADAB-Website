SAKADAB

I lab.

"Kung kaya nila, kaya rin natin"

SAKADAB or the Samahang may mga iba't ibang Kakayanan sa Dabaw
is an organization aimed to help PWD's or Persons with Disabilities who wanted to be self-sufficient
in their livelihood and take control of their lives.

They are situated in Los Amigos, Tugbok District, Davao City near the Barangay Hall.
They are currently selling their merchandise such as the Philippine Flag beaded wallet.
They were also the ones who competed in the Kadayawan Fashion Show with their scarf.
As Group 5, we strived to profile their status and how their system worked.
