    • | Objectives |


    • To inspire, help, and train other people with the same limitations as them.



  • To show people that despite their disabilities, they are just as capable and talented as any

         other able-bodied person.



  • Are able to live and sustain themselves without help.




    • | Factors for Creation |


  • Lack of freedom to do anything independently.



  • Lack of accessibility.




    • | SAKADAB's Issues |


  • Short budgets and are unable to pay for their essential items (Food, Electricity, Medicine).



  • New competition for their businesses which limit their influence in the area.

    • | Founders of SAKADAB |

  • The formerly 27 members, now 18 currently, were all the founders of the institution.



  • President: Ma’am Marisol Magalasin



  • Vice President: Debra Estreba



  • Members: Lola Edel,

        Kuya Rey,

        Sir Janito and many more.




    • | Aims and Aspirations |

  • Aim to support each other as well as others with

          the same situation as them.



  • Enhance their community in all aspects.



  • Make people happy through their different

          activities and business.




    • | SAKADAB Member's Context |


  • Besides the first 27 members, they are not

          really accepting anybody new to join the Sakadab community.



  • Most of them came from a previous institution called “Lady of Victory” in Davao.



  • Everyone in the community are people with disabilities.



  • They asked help from the government and accepted donations to help

          in building and sustaining their institution.



  • Some of them suffer severely, which is why they need proper medical

          assistance from the government of Davao.

    • | Supporting Organizations & Institutions |


  • The Davao Government usually provides support and needs for them.



  • Davao light also supports them through donating needed materials and

         tools for their business.

        Mixers for their bakery and sewing machines for handicrafts.



  • SAKADAB’s “bahay” was also created through ABS-CBN after a ground-breaking incident in

        2012.

  • The Ateneo de Davao Junior High School is also an institution currently supporting their

          community.

    | Advocacy Programs |


  • They implement fund-raisers like concerts for the public to watch.



  • They also have a facebook page for where we are able to learn and reach them.



  • Every person has to contribute equally to the community.

        They each have a job or activities to do to help and support the institutions.
